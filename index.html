Crystal Game!

Welcome to the Crystal Game! Here you will be given a randomly generated number that you will need to match to win the game. To work your score up from zero, you will need to click on each of the crystals, adding them together to increase your score. Be careful though, if your score becomes higher than the target number, the game is lost! But don't worry, The game will reset whether you win or lose, so you can keep trying as many times as you like! Each crystal has a random number, and you won't be told exactly what it is. But with a bit of observation, logic, and a good memory, you will figure it out! Good luck and Have fun!
